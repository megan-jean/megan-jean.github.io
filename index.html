Hi there.

I’m an Austin-based, multi-disciplinary Product Designer who focuses on design research, interface aesthetics, and user-centered design interactions. I am currently attending school for UI/UX Design at the University of Texas, Austin, McCombs School of Business. I am of the firm belief that the definition of great user experience is a design that goes completely unnoticed by the user. Great design is seamless, never hinders a user, and should be easily facilitated by people of all backgrounds and abilities in our new digital world. When I'm not working, you can most likely catch me reading, drawing, enjoying a local dive, cooking with the freshest ingredients I can find, or traveling around South East Asia. I'm currently reading About Face: The Essentials of Interaction Design by Alan Cooper, and thoroughly enjoying reruns of the undisputed classic, Planet Earth.
